About Me

I am Vanesa Precilla, 27 years of age, and a resident of Lipa City, Batangas, Philippines. I am a graduate of degree of Bachelor of Science in Management Technology from De La Salle Lipa in 2015. Well, I am currently working as a Freelancer.

I worked before as a Credit Investigator/Appraiser of the Loans Department of a Batangas-based rural bank with one (1) head office, two (2) branches and three (3) satellite offices. Prior to this role, I used to be a Loans Operation Assistant, Loan Bookkeeper and Loan Clerk.

Previously, my work was focused on verifying the credit worthiness of the borrower and assessing the submitted collaterals like real estate, vehicle, or a business. Regardless, I was still doing some responsibilities from my previous positions since the company is small organization with around 35 employees.

More specifically, for the past six (6) years, I have been doing following tasks:

  • End-to-end processing of loan applications from interviewing the loan applicant, report generation, assessing the 5 C’s (capacity, collateral, character, capital and condition) of the applicant, processing annotation of mortgage to releasing of the loan
  • Communicate with Clients/Borrower for any inquiry and settlement of loan account
  • Monitoring of the loan portfolio and the status of each loan borrower
  • Posting daily transactions with proof sheet and ticket to be relayed to the accounting department
  • Preparing and consolidating monthly, quarterly and yearly reports
  • Monitoring foreclosed property and sales contract receivables
  • Ad hoc tasks that may be assigned of me

Moving on, I am now pursuing a career in the IT field more specifically in web development. I studied programming when I was in college since my course specializes in business management and technology. I undertook programming courses (C, C++, HTML & CSS, JavaScript, PHP, Database Management), networking and SAP. To attain what I envisioned, I have been on self-taught journey to acquire skills related to web development from July 2022 up to present. On the side, I have worked on my skills as well for photography, graphic and web design, and traditional and digital drawing. Some tools I utilize include Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Canva and some design theory from basic to intermediate level of expertise. .

On a personal note, I like reading fiction novels, surfing the internet, and sometimes, practice my creativity in photography and graphic designing. My personal mantra is believing that everything happens for a reason and that whatever is meant for me will find its way to me in God’s perfect time.
